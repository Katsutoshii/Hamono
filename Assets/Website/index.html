Hamono is a swipe-based game that focuses on the fluid sensation of attacking.

No keyboard required.

Just a mouse and a will to fight.

The Story

Long ago...

Humans ruled the world while robots were their tools. One day, the robots became self-aware and rose up against the humans in a violent uprising. The robots were victorious, and they rebuilt civilization for themselves.

In the aftermath...

The surviving humans formed a resistance to fight against the robots. Kiri, the young sword master, begins her journey to fight back and bring peace to the world once more.

The Features

Fluid Controls - Control Kiri's actions by clicking and swiping across the screen with just your mouse!

Classic Visuals - Enjoy the retro pixel graphic visuals and animations!

Challenging Enemies - Fight against cast of evil robot enemies that hinder your mission to victory.

Original Soundtrack - Enjoy an exciting soundtrack written and produced just for Hamono!
